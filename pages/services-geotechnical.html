Geotechnical Investigations

We undertake Geotechnical reporting to support Building Consent and Resource Consent applications. We carry out shallow subsoil testing, to determine the soil profile and the capacity to support building foundations and retaining wall structures for new builds (residential and light commercial) alterations, additions and Sale and Purchase agreements.
